Title: Kosi Agbara, Toda Biti Jesu


Kosi agbara, toda biti Jesu
( There's no power like that of Jesus)

Kosi agbara, toda biti baba
( There's no power like that of our king)

Kosi agbara to da biti Jesu
( There's no power like that of Jesus)

Agbara agbara agbara to ju agbara lo
( His power is greater than every other  power)
