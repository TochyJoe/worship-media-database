Title: We Declare Your Majesty


Verse 1
We declare Your majesty
We proclaim that Your name is exalted

For You reign, magnificently rule
Victoriously and Your power’s

Shown through all the earth

Verse 1
And we exclaim, our God is mighty
We lift up Your name, for You are holy

We sing it again, all honor and glory
In adoration

We bow before Your throne
