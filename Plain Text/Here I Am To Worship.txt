Title: Here I Am To Worship


LIGHT OF THE WORLD
You STEPPED DOWN

INTO DARKNESS
OPEN MY EYES

LET ME SEE

BEAUTY THAT MAKES
THIS HEART ADORE THEE

HOPE OF A LIFE
SPENT WITH You

HERE I AM TO WORSHIP
HERE I AM TO BOW DOWN

HERE I AM TO SAY THAT
You ARE MY God

You ARE ALL TOGETHER WORTHY
ALL TOGETHER LOVELY

ALL TOGETHER WONDERFUL TO ME

I'LL NEVER KNOW
HOW MUCH IT COSTS

TO SEE MY SINS UPON
THE CROSS
